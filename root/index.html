What is CPD?

CPD stands for Continuing Professional Development. It involves tracking and documenting the skills, knowledge and experience that are gained both formally and informally as you work, beyond any initial training.

To summarise, it is a record of what you experience, learn and apply.

How does it work?

Currently the specification is that CPD must be evidenced sufficiently, whether by a confirmation email, screenshots or both. The grades are:

  • 10 hours of CPD engagement: 3LOW.
  • 20 hours of CPD engagement: 2.1HIGH.
  • 30 hours of CPD engagement: 1EXC.
